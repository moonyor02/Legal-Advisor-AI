Title: The Sixth Schedule of the Constitution of India (Selected Provisions)

[STATUTORY DATA: CONSTITUTIONAL FRAMEWORK]

1. Paragraph 1 — Autonomous Districts (1) The tribal areas in each item of Parts I, II, IIA and III of the table appended to paragraph 20 of this Schedule shall be autonomous districts. (3) The Governor may, by public notification, include or exclude any area, create new districts, or increase/diminish the area of any autonomous district.

2. Paragraph 2 — Constitution of Councils (1) There shall be a District Council for each autonomous district consisting of not more than thirty members. (3) Each District Council shall be a body corporate by the name of "the District Council of (name of district)", shall have perpetual succession and a common seal and shall by the said name sue and be sued.

3. Paragraph 3 — Legislative Powers over Land (1) The District Council for an autonomous district shall have power to make laws with respect to: (a) The allotment, occupation or use, or the setting apart, of land, other than any land which is a reserved forest, for the purposes of agriculture or grazing or for residential or other non-agricultural purposes or for any other purpose likely to promote the interests of the inhabitants of any village or town.

4. Paragraph 20 — Tribal Areas (Part I)

The North Cachar Hills District.

The Karbi Anglong District.

The Bodoland Territorial Area District (BTAD).
