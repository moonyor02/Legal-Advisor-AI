DOCUMENT TYPE: GOVERNMENT VS. CITIZEN
CONTEXT: KHAS LAND AND VGR/PGR EVICTION

1. TYPES OF GOVERNMENT LAND:
- KHAS LAND: Unallotted government land.
- VGR/PGR: Village or Professional Grazing Reserves. Occupation here is strictly prohibited.

2. EVICTION DEFENSE (RULE 18):
The Government can evict occupants of VGR land without long notice.
- STRATEGY: If the user is an 'Indigenous Landless Person,' they should petition the BTC (Bodoland Territorial Council) for 'Land Allotment' under the State Land Policy.
- LOOPHOLE: If the user has occupied the land for over 15 years, they may be eligible for 'Rehabilitation' before being evicted.
