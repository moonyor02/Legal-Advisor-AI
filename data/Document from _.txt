Title: The Assam Forest Regulation, 1891 (Forest Governance & Enforcement)

Metadata:

Statute: Regulation 7 of 1891

Authority: Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (PCCF), Assam / BTC Forest Dept

Key Locations: Ultapani RF, Chirang RF, Rengma RF (Uriamghat)

Status: Actively enforced via 2025 High Court Directives

Key Statutory Provisions
Section 24 & 25 — Penalties for Trespass:

Prohibits "fresh clearing" of forest land and any unauthorized felling, cutting, or girdling of trees.

Possession of forest land without a settlement order is a criminal offense.

Section 28 — Power to Evict:

Grants the Forest Department the power to remove any person who has "unlawfully entered" a reserved forest.

Includes the power to demolish structures and seize crops/produce grown on forest land.

Section 49 — Confiscation:

Empowers forest officers to seize vehicles, tools, and boats used in forest offenses. Appeals against such orders lie with the District Judge.
